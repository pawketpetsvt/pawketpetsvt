✧ About PawketPetsVT

PawketPetsVT is a fan-interactive pet game built around the VTuber group of the same name! Inspired by the pet games of the early 2000s (think Neopets vibes ✨), collect, raise, and care for little virtual versions of your favourite streamers.


Earn 🪙 PawketPoints by playing minigames, watching streams, and interacting with your pets. The more you engage — here and on Twitch — the more your pets will grow! 🌟
