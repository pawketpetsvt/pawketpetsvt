About PawketPetsVT

PawketPetsVT is a fan-interactive pet game inspired by the pet games of the early 2000s. Collect, raise, and care for little virtual versions of your favourite streamers.


Earn PawketPoints by playing minigames, watching streams, and interacting with your pets!
