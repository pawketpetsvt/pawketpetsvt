✧ About PawketPetsVT

PawketPetsVT is a fan-interactive pet game built around the VTuber group of the same name! Inspired by the pet games of the early 2000s (think Neopets vibes ✨), this is a place where the community can collect, raise, and care for little virtual versions of their favourite streamers.


Earn 🪙 PawketPoints by playing minigames, watching streams, and interacting with your pets. Spend them in the shop to buy items, toys, and snacks for your collection. The more you engage — here and on Twitch — the more your pets will grow! 🌟
